About NGO


- VISION OF TRANSFORMING NATION is a non-governmental organization (NGO). Registered under Societies Registration Act. 1860(Act xx1 of 1860) on 14th September 2021.

- Prime objective of VISION OF TRANSFORMING NATION BHANDARA is to encourage country's youths and other children to complete their studies.

- Spreading awareness among citizens regarding to their education , their rights as well as their duties or responsibilities for our Nation.

- To build Self confidence and encourage the youths. Specially girls for come forward and fight for their rights as well as NGO help them for increasing their guts and help them to set the goal for their future.

- NGO wants to aware citizens about their social hygiene as well as their responsibilities for their area's Cleanliness.

- To protect our nature by making green and clean city with the help of youths.
